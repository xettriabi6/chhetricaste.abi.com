KATAWAL


They were one of subcaste of the Tagadhari Chhetri community. They were considered as one of a noble family in Chhetri clan throughout the Nepalese history of Gorkha Kingdom. They belong to Jharra Chhetri (a subgroup of the Chhetri caste).
Varient of this surname(katawal, katowal, katuwal)
