BOGATI

Bogati is a surname found in Nepal. They belong to the highest of the Chhetri community in Nepal. Mostly scattered around western Nepal and of military background. Some[who?] believe Gorkha to be their home land. Several families of whom came with the conquest of Kathmandu reside in the valley mainly in Lalitpur. Notable people with the surname include.

      BABU BOGATI (nepali singer and actor)
      POST BAHADUR BOGATI (1953-2014) nepalese politician
      SABITRI BOGATI ( nepalese politician)
      TIKA BOGATI (nepalese athlete)
