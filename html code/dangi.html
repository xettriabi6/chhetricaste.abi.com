DANGI

The Dangi are an agricultural Hindu caste native to northern India. Their traditional occupation is farming. They speak several languages, but Hindi is the most commonly understood language among them.
 The Dangi surname is mostly found in India and Nepal. They are mostly Hindu people, they are well flourished in central part of Nepal, like Dang district, and eastern part of Nepal. They are often known as DC or Dangi Kshetri. They are a pure kshatriya tribe and kaushika gotra.
